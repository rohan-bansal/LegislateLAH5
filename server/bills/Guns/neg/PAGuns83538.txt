Highlights: Prohibits municipalities and counties from regulating the lawful ownership, possession, transfer, or transportation of firearms, ammunition, or ammunition components, or from taking action against any firearm or ammunition manufacturer (Sec. 1). Authorizes any individual who was adversely affected by any municipal or county ordinance, resolution, or rule found in violation of the aforementioned provisions to seek declaratory and injunctive relief for actual damages attributable to the violation in the appropriate court (Sec. 1).
