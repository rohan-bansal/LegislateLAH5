Highlights: Establishes Delaware Firearm Transaction Approval Program for the purpose of conducting background checks (Sec. 1). Requires Delaware Police State Bureau of Identification to conduct all background checks required for the sale or transfer of firearms using the above program (Sec. 1). Requires the State Bureau of Identification to conform with existing privacy regulations when managing firearm background check data (Sec. 1). Establishes a hotline for firearm licensees to report background check violations (Sec. 2). Requires the State Bureau of Identification to transmit information to the FBI for use in federal background check programs (Sec. 2).
