Highlights: Requires individuals to report lost or stolen firearms to a local law-enforcement agency within 48 hours after such individual is made aware of the loss or theft (Sec. 1). Requires the law-enforcement agency to enter the report information into the National Crime Information Center maintained by the Federal Bureau of Investigation (Sec. 1). Establishes a civil penalty of not more than $250 for violations of this section (Sec. 1).
