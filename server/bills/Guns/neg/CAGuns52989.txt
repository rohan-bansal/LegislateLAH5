Highlights: Prohibits an individual with a concealed carry license from possessing a concealed firearm on the grounds of a public or private school or within 1,000 feet of a public or private school (Sec. 1). Prohibits an individual from possessing a firearm on the grounds of a campus of a public or private university or college, unless it is with the written permission of the university or college president or equivalent university or college authority (Sec. 1). Exempts honorably retired peace officers from the provision of this bill (Sec. 1).
