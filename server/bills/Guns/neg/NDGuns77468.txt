Highlights: Prohibits the application of federal laws and regulations to firearms, accessories and ammunition manufactured and used within the state (Sec. 1). Specifies that generic and insignificant parts that have other manufacturing or consumer product applications are not subject to regulation by congress (Sec. 1). Specifies that accessories imported from other states into the state of North Dakota will not be subject to federal laws and regulations because they will be attached to a gun within the state (Sec. 1).
