Highlights: Prohibits ownership of a firearm by an individual who has been previously convicted of domestic violence harassment (Sec. 1). Specifies that an individual who owns a firearm and has been previously convicted of domestic violence is guilty of the crime of unlawful possession of a firearm in the second degree (Sec. 1). Classifies unlawful possession of a firearm in the second degree as a class C felony (Sec. 1).
