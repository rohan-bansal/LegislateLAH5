Highlights: Classifies the ownership, purchase, or possession of a firearm within 10 years of the second of 2 of any of the following convictions as an infraction resulting in a maximum fine of $250 (Sec. 2): Possession of a controlled substance with intent to sell; Under the influence of a controlled substance; Vehicle manslaughter while intoxicated; Public intoxication; Driving under the influence of alcohol or drugs; Driving under the influence and causing injury; or Driving under the influence of alcohol while on probation for prior driving under the influence offense. Prohibits an individual who has been ordered by a court to obtain assisted outpatient treatment from purchasing, receiving, possessing, or controlling any firearm or deadly weapon while subject to assisted outpatient treatment (Sec. 4).
