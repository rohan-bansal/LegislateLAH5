Highlights: Requires any law enforcement officer that is arresting or citing a person for domestic assault to confiscate any firearms that are (Sec. 1): In the immediate possession or control of the person being arrested or cited; In plain view of the officer; or Discovered during a consensual search. Requires law-enforcement agencies to return firearms within 5 days, except when the owner has been convicted of a firearm-related felony or is considered a domestic threat (Sec. 1).
