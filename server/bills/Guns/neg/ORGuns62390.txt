Highlights: Prohibits an individual from possessing a firearm if such individual is the subject of a restraining order held by a family or household member of the individual, a child of a family or household member, or a child of the individual (Sec. 1). Prohibits an individual from possessing a firearm if such individual is the subject of a court order that finds the individual presents a physical threat to a family or household member of the individual, a child of a family or household member, or a child of the individual (Sec. 1). Prohibits an individual from possessing a firearm if such individual has been convicted of certain misdemeanors and was at the time a family or household member of the victim or a parent or guardian of the victim (Sec. 1). Prohibits an individual from possessing a firearm if such individual has been convicted of stalking (Sec. 1).
