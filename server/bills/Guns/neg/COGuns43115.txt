Highlights: Prohibits the sale, transfer, or possession of a large-capacity magazine (Sec. 1). Defines “large-capacity magazine” as a fixed or detachable magazine or similar device that is designed to be readily converted to accept more than 15 rounds of ammunition or a shotgun that can accept more than 28 inches of shotgun shells (Sec. 1). Authorizes an individual to retain possession of a large-capacity magazine if the individual owns the magazine on July 1, 2013 and maintains continuous possession of the magazine (Sec. 1). Exempts certain entities from the provisions of this bill including, but not limited to, the following (Sec. 1): Branches of the armed forces of the United States; Departments, agencies, or political subdivisions of Colorado or the United State government; and Firearms retailers who possess the large-capacity magazines for sales conducted outside the state. Requires manufacturers to permanently mark all large-capacity magazines to indicate that the magazines were manufactured or assembled after July 1, 2013 (Sec. 1). Classifies a violation of the provisions of this bill as a Class 2 misdemeanor (Sec. 1).
