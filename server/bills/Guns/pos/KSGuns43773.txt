Highlights: Prohibits the enforcement of any federal firearm regulation which violates the second amendment to the constitution of the United States (Sec. 6). Prohibits an employee of the state of Kansas or the United States government from enforcing a regulation on a firearm that was manufactured and owned in the state of Kansas and has remained in the state of Kansas (Secs. 6 & 7). Specifies that the provisions of the bill only apply to firearms, firearm accessories, and ammunition that were manufactured and owned in the state of Kansas and have remained in the state of Kansas on or after October 1, 2009 (Sec. 10). Classifies a violation of this bill as a level 10 nonperson felony (Sec. 7). Specifies that any provision of this bill determined to be invalid is severable from the other provisions of this bill (Sec.11).
