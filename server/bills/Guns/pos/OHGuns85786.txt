Highlights: Authorizes the arming of certain persons within school safety zones (Sec. 1). Authorizes said persons (ex. school employees) to bypass certain training requirements (Sec. 1). Authorizes the Attorney General to adopt new rules regarding the bearing of arms in school safety zones (Sec. 1). Repeals Ohio Supreme Court decision Gabbard v. Madison Local School Dist. Bd. of Edn. (Sec. 1).
