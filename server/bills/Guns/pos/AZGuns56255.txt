Highlights: Authorizes an individual with a valid permit to carry a “deadly weapon” on his or her person in a public establishment or at a public event, except in locations including, but not limited to, the following locations (Sec. 1): A “secured facility”; An educational institution; and A state, county, or municipal judicial department. Defines “secured facility” as a building with security measures, including biometric or coded entry or an electronic weapons screening device or security personnel at each entrance (Sec. 1).
