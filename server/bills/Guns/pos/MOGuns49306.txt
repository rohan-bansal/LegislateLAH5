Highlights: Authorizes a state employee to keep a firearm in the individual’s vehicle on state property if the vehicle is locked, the firearm is not visible, and the individual is conducting activities within the scope of his or her employment (Sec. A). Prohibits a county, municipality, or other governmental body from participating in a program in which individuals are given a “thing of value” in exchange for surrendering his or her firearm, except under the following circumstances (Sec. A): The county, municipality, or governmental body has adopted an ordinance authorizing their participation in such program; and The ordinance requires a firearm received to be offered for sale or trade to at least 2 licensed firearm dealers prior to being destroyed.
