Highlights: Repeals the provision that authorizes a county or municipality to prohibit an individual with a handgun carry permit from possessing a handgun in a public park, nature trail, campground, or other similar public place owned or operated by the state, county, or municipality (Sec. 1). Authorizes an individual with a handgun carry permit to possess a handgun on the grounds of the state capitol and the surrounding capitol complex (Sec. 3).
