Highlights: Prohibits state agencies or law enforcement from enforcing federal regulations that (Sec. 2): Impose restrictions or prohibitions on gun ownership; Require firearm owners to be on a registry; Require background checks for purchasing a firearm; or Establish firearm buyback programs. Prohibits any state agency that enforces any federal firearms regulations specified above from receiving funds (Sec. 2). Authorizes citizens to file a complaint with the attorney general if they have evidence that a state political subdivision is enforcing federal firearms laws (Sec. 2).
