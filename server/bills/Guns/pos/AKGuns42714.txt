Highlights: Exempts firearms, firearm accessories, and ammunition possessed in Alaska from any federal firearm regulation, whereas current law only exempts firearms manufactured in the state (Sec. 2). Authorizes the state attorney general to defend any citizen of Alaska who is prosecuted by the United States government in regards to federal law that regulates firearms (Sec. 4). Classifies the enforcement of any federal law that restricts firearms in the following ways as a Class C felony (Sec. 5): Limits the ownership of a semi-automatic firearm or firearm magazine; or Requires the registration of a firearm, firearm accessory, or magazine.
