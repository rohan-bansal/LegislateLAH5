Highlights: Prohibits the governing body of a city, county, or metropolitan government from establishing an ordinance that prohibits an individual from possessing, carrying, or storing a handgun or ammunition in the following areas (Sec. 1): A public park; A playground; A civic center; or A public building or property used for recreational purposes. Repeals the requirement that the chief administrator of a public property display a sign that states the maximum penalty for carrying a gun on public recreational property (Sec. 2).
