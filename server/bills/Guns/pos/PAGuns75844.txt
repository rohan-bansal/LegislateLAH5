Highlights: Classifies shooting ranges; sportsman clubs; hunting facilities; firearm and ammunition product manufacturers, retailers, importers, and distributors; and their employees and agents as life-sustaining businesses (Sec 2). Prohibits these operations from being shuttered or limited in their ability to produce, provide, sell, or otherwise offer (Sec 2): Firearms; Ammunition; Firearm accessories; Component parts of firearms; Ammunition and firearm accessories; and All services, training, safety, and practices related to firearms and hunting.
