Highlights: Prohibits individuals from knowingly carrying a firearm on public school property including, buildings, grounds, school buses, or vans Sec. 1). Exempts the following individuals and groups from the provisions of this act, including (Sec. 1): Any individual picking up or dropping off a student whose firearm remains in their motor vehicle; Any individual authorized in writing by the school board to possess a firearm; and Any duly appointed law enforcement officer, properly trained school resource officer, or member of the armed forces. Specifies that this act will take effect immediately upon passage (Sec. 1).
