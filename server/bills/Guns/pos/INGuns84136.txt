Highlights: Specifies policy regarding carriage of handguns by parole officers (Sec. 2). Repeals certain handgun license requirements (Sec. 3). Establishes unlawful carrying of handgun as crime (Sec. 4). Increases firearm theft from Level 6 felony classification to Level 5 (Sec. 7). Defines “mental defective”, “alien”, other terms (Sec. 9). Authorizes adult persons to carry handgun w/o license unless otherwise prohibited (Sec. 12).
