Highlights: Establishes that any federal law which violates the Second Amendment is null and void in the state of Tennessee (Sec. 1). Prohibits the state from using public funds, personnel, or property to enforce, administer, or cooperate with the enforcement or administration of any law, treaty, executive order, rule, or regulation of the US government that violates the Second Amendment of the US Constitution (Sec. 1). Specifies that this bill shall take effect immediately upon becoming law (Sec. 2).
