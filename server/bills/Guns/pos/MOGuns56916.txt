Highlights: Authorizes an individual to carry a concealed weapon without a permit, except in areas where firearms are restricted (Sec. 1). Authorizes guests of a property owner to use deadly force against an individual who unlawfully enters said property (Sec. 1). Repeals the requirement that an individual retreat from a location he or she is authorized to be in before using deadly force to defend him or herself from an individual using unlawful force (Sec. 1).
