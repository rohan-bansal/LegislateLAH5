Highlights: Repeals state prohibition against manufacturing, importing, selling, or possessing a “sawed-off shotgun” (Sec. 4). Defines “sawed-off shotgun” as meeting the following criteria (Sec. 2): A shotgun having 1 or more barrels less than 18 inches in length; or A shotgun made into a modified weapon that is less than 26 inches in length. Authorizes the court to add sentences of imprisonment of not more than 10 years if an individual knowingly or intentionally uses or possesses a sawed-off shotgun in violation of federal law while committing an offense of dealing in a controlled substance (Sec. 5).
