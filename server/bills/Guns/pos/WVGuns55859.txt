Highlights: Authorizes an individual 21 years or older to carry a concealed deadly weapon without a permit if the following conditions are met (Sec. 1): The individual is a United States citizen or legal resident; and The individual is not prohibited from receiving, possessing or transporting a firearm. Classifies the unauthorized possession of a concealed weapon without a permit for an individual under 21 years old with the following violations (Sec. 1): A misdemeanor with a fine from $100 to $1,000 and possible imprisonment up to 12 months for the first offense; and A felony with a fine from $1,000 to $5,000 and imprisonment of 1 to 5 years for a subsequent offense. Authorizes an individual between 18 to 21 years old to apply for a state license to carry a concealed weapon (Sec. 1).
